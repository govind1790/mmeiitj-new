Research Scholars' Day 2023


February 18-19, 2023

09:00 AM - 06:00 PM

IME Department, C5 Lecture Hall


"Without the dreamers who write science fiction and other imaginary material we'd still be sitting in caves… if we weren't already extinct."   - William C. Samples

RSD 2023

The Materials Science and Engineering Department at IIT Kanpur is organizing a two-day symposium entitled Research Scholar Day (RSD 2023) from February 18–19, 2023. Research Scholar Day aims to recognize and celebrate the cutting-edge research carried out by the Research Scholars of the department. The two-day conference includes exciting technical workshops, talks by eminent speakers, as well as oral and poster presentations by scholars on their ongoing research. This colloquium provides an excellent platform for researchers to share their views, express their ideas, exchange knowledge, and learn from the experience through enhanced awareness and meaningful interaction.

RSD includes these major events:

Faculty Talks Oral Presentation Poster Presentation Workshops
3 Minute Thesis (3MT) Metallography Contest Metabuzz Memellurgy
